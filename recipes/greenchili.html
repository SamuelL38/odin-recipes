Bailey's Green Chili

Most Excellent Green Chili made by my wife!

Using chopped pork, green chiles, hominy and veggies, you can make this most excellent chili too!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Pounds Chopped Pork
  • 2 Pounts Green Chiles
  • Can o' Hominy
  • Cup o' Veggies

Steps:

  1. Cook the meat with chopped onions and veggies
  2. Add green chiles
  3. Add hominy at the end
