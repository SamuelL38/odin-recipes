Sammy's Spicy Tacos

Spicy Tacos made by me!

Using pulled pork, spices and jalapenos, you can make these most excellent tacos too!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Pounds Pulled Pork
  • 2 Jalapenos
  • Various Spices
  • Tortillas

Steps:

  1. Cook the meat with chopped onions
  2. Add diced peppers
  3. Add spices and fry the tortillas
