Mom's Red Chili

Delicious Bourbon Chili made by my Mom!

Using ground beef, bison, beans and bourbon, you can make this most excellent chili too!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Pounds Ground Beef
  • 2 Pounds Ground Bison
  • Can o' Beans
  • Cup o' Bourbon ( the cheap stuff ;) )

Steps:

  1. Cook the meat with chopped onions
  2. Add Bourbon
  3. Add Beans at the end
